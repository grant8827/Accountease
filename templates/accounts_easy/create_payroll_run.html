What happens when you create a payroll run?

  • Automatic payslip generation: Payslips will be created for ALL active employees in your business
  • Bonus inclusion: Monthly bonuses for the payroll period will be automatically included in calculations
  • Tax calculations: All tax deductions (PAYE, NIS, NHT, HEART, Education) will be calculated based on your payroll configuration
  • YTD updates: Year-to-date (YTD) records will be updated for all employees
  • Bonus tracking: Applied bonuses will be marked as processed and cannot be modified
  • Net pay calculation: Final net pay will be calculated automatically (Gross Pay - Total Deductions)
